ABOUT US

Dr. Karan Shah

Dr. Karan Shah is a distinguished Consultant Psychiatrist, De-Addiction Expert, and Sexologist with extensive experience and expertise. He holds an MBBS and an MD in Psychiatry and is a respected member of the Indian Psychiatric Society. Dr. Shah has treated over 35,000 patients across Gujarat, Rajasthan, and Punjab, demonstrating his ongoing commitment to improving mental health nationwide. His contributions to the National Mental Health Program in Mehsana and Vishnagar civil hospitals under the Government of Gujarat are particularly noteworthy.

In addition to his clinical practice, Dr. Shah is a dedicated educator. He imparts his knowledge of psychiatry to MBBS and physiotherapy students and delivers numerous impactful lectures on mental health awareness. Recognizing the importance of accessibility, Dr. Shah also provides online consultations to patients across India.

Dr. Shah's compassionate and supportive nature continues to bring hope and healing to many, making him a trusted and beloved figure in the field of mental health by going beyond the stigma ,addressing the invisible battle and navigating its challenges.
